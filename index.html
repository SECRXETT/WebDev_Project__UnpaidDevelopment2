Car of the Day: Mazda RX-7

The Mazda RX-7 is what happens when a sports car decides it wants to be both a lightweight feather and a tiny angry vacuum cleaner. Powered by Mazda’s famousl rotary engine, basically a spinning Dorito of fury, it revs high, sounds wild, and drinks fuel like it’s happy hour. Sleek, low, and unmistakably 80s–90s cool, the RX-7 earned its cult status by being lightweight, perfectly balanced, and surprisingly friendly to drive, until you push the boost too far and it reminds you who’s in charge.
